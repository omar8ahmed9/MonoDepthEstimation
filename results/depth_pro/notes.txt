Model: Apple Depth Pro

Evaluation split:
- Used the official split from data/splits/endovis/test_files.txt
- Evaluated on 551 test samples

Dataset / adapter details:
- Input images were read from the prepared SCARED dataset layout:
  SCARED_prepared/{train|test}/datasetX/keyframeY/data/left/<frame_id>.png
- Frame paths were mapped from the official split entries of the form:
  datasetX/keyframeY frame_id l

Ground truth:
- Ground truth depth was taken from EndoMUST/splits/endovis/gt_depths.npz
- Verified that EndoMUST test_files.txt matches the official benchmark test_files.txt exactly before reuse

Prediction details:
- Depth Pro inference was run on each RGB image
- Predicted depth maps were resized to ground-truth resolution before metric computation
- Predictions were clipped to [1e-3, 150.0]

Reporting setting:
- Results are raw predictions
- No median scaling
- No scale alignment
